Our Products

A comprehensive range of industrial valves engineered for reliability across all critical process applications.

01
Ball Valves

Full bore and reduced bore ball valves in SS, CS, and brass for high-pressure and high-temperature industrial applications.

02
Gate Valves

Heavy-duty gate valves for on/off pipeline control. Multiple sizes and pressure ratings for industrial and commercial use.

03
Globe Valves

Precision globe valves for excellent flow regulation and throttling in steam, water, oil, and chemical services.

04
Butterfly Valves

Lightweight, cost-effective butterfly valves for large-diameter pipelines with quick quarter-turn operation.

05
Check Valves

Non-return check valves preventing backflow. Swing, lift, and dual plate configurations available.

06
Needle & Plug Valves

Precision needle and plug valves for fine flow control in instrumentation, sampling, and process systems.
